How does family sharing work?

  1. Invite family members via code generated by app when you create family
  2. They create their account
  3. You all see shared budgets
  4. For premium family, each person can upload their own statements
  5. Combined view shows total family finances

Premium Family: Up to 5 family members, shared budgets, and collaborative tracking.

Can I control what family members see?

No - and that's by design! The purpose of Family Budget is full financial transparency. When you invite someone to your family:

  • They see ALL family accounts, transactions, and budgets
  • Everyone has the same view of family finances
  • This encourages open communication about money
  • Each member can still filter the dashboard by person to see individual spending
If you need privacy: Use Premium Solo instead. Family Budget is for families who want complete transparency.

What if I want to remove a family member?

Family members can remove themselves from family. Family owner can remove family members from family settings. Their transactions, accounts, budgets, goals, and settings stay with them.

Do you support multiple currencies?

Yes! 15+ currencies including:

  • Each account can have its own currency
  • Dashboard shows totals per currency (no forced conversion)

What's the difference between Physical and Virtual goals?

  • Physical Goals: Money in a separate account (e.g., savings account)
    • You manually transfer money
    • We track if you've transferred this cycle
  • Virtual Goals: Money allocated in your mind (still in checking)
    • No physical transfer needed
    • We deduct from "Safe to Spend"

Do you have a mobile app?

Not yet! But our web app is fully mobile-responsive. Works great on phones/tablets. Native apps planned for 2026.
