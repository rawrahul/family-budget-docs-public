üöÄ Getting Started with Family Budget Tracker

Welcome! This guide will help you set up your account and start tracking your finances in under 10 minutes.

üí° Tip: You can start with the Free Solo plan and upgrade to Premium anytime to unlock AI statement upload and unlimited features.

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Go to family-budget.digraw.xyz
  2. Click "Sign Up"
  3. Enter your email and create a password
  4. Verify your email (check spam folder if needed)
  5. Log in to your new account
‚úÖ Free Trial: Premium plans include a one-time 7-day free trial. Try all features risk-free!

Step 2: Set Up Your Pay Cycle

Tell us when you get paid so we can track your budget accurately.

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Pay Cycle Settings
  2. Choose your pay frequency:
    • Weekly - Every 7 days
    • Bi-weekly - Every 14 days
    • Monthly - Once per month
    • Custom - Any number of days
  3. Set your cycle start day (e.g., "1" for 1st of month, or day of week)
  4. Click "Save"
üí° Why this matters: Your dashboard shows spending for the current pay cycle, not just the calendar month. This gives you a more accurate view of your budget!

Step 3: Add Your Bank Accounts

Add all accounts you want to track (checking, savings, credit cards).

  1. Go to Accounts in the navigation
  2. Click "+ Add Account"
  3. Fill in:
    • Account Name (e.g., "Chase Checking")
    • Account Type (Bank Account or Credit Card)
    • Currency (USD, GBP, EUR, etc.)
    • Initial Balance (optional - current balance)
  4. Click "Add Account"
  5. Repeat for all your accounts
üåç Multi-Currency: Each account can have its own currency. Perfect for international families!

Step 4: Upload Your First Statement (Premium)

Let AI extract transactions automatically from your bank statement.

‚ö†Ô∏è Premium Feature: AI Statement Upload requires Premium Solo ($9.99/mo) or Premium Family ($14.99/mo). Free users can add transactions manually.

For Premium Users:

  1. Go to AI Statement Upload
  2. Click "Upload Statement"
  3. Select your account
  4. Upload your PDF or image statement
  5. Wait 10-30 seconds for AI processing
  6. Review extracted transactions
  7. Click "Confirm & Add"

For Free Users:

  1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Add ‚Üí Transaction

  3. Click "Add Transaction"
üí° AI Accuracy: Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy on standard bank statements. It learns from your corrections!

Step 5: Set Budget Limits

Define spending limits for each category.

  1. Go to Budget ‚Üí Budget Limits
  2. Click "+ Add Budget Limit"
  3. Select a category (e.g., "Groceries")
  4. Set your monthly limit
  5. Click "Save"
  6. Repeat for other categories
üìä Dashboard View: Your dashboard shows how much you've spent vs your limits in real-time!

Step 6: Create Savings Goals

Set goals and track your progress automatically.

  1. Go to Budget ‚Üí Savings Goals
  2. Click "+ Create Goal"
  3. Choose goal type:
    • Physical Goal - Money in a separate savings account
    • Virtual Goal - Mental allocation (money stays in checking)
  4. Fill in:
    • Goal Name (e.g., "Emergency Fund")
    • Target Amount
    • Per-Cycle Allocation (how much to save each pay cycle)
    • Deduction Day (when to save each cycle)
  5. Click "Create Goal"
üí° Physical vs Virtual: Physical goals track actual transfers to savings. Virtual goals reduce your "Safe to Spend" amount without moving money.

  4. Copy the invite code
  5. Share the code with family members
  6. They sign up and enter the code
  7. Everyone now sees shared budgets!
